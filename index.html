What is ROS?

Framework for robotic research and development

  • Distributed node based communication and message service
       IOT!
  • Device Drivers
       Cameras, Arms, Sensors
  • UI based graphics and simulation

Who Uses ROS?

  • Universities.....Of Course!
  • Hobby Communities
  • The Government
  • Industry
    • ⋮
  • Pretty much everyone!

ROS Development

9 year of development!

  • 14 million lines of code

  • 1000's of packages and drivers
  • ROS 2.0 and H-ROS on the horizon!
